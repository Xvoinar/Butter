Privacy Policy – Masło

Last updated: 2025-08-06

1. General Information

This privacy policy describes what data is collected and processed in the mobile game Masło, available on Google Play, and for what purposes it is used.

2. What data we collect

The game uses Firebase Authentication to create an anonymous player account — without asking for email, name, or any personal information.

  • Anonymous user identifier (Firebase UID)
  • Username chosen by the player
  • Gameplay progress data: number of clicks, rebirths
  • Information about public matchmaking availability (public nickname)

3. Purpose of data processing

  • Saving and restoring player progress
  • Allowing matchmaking and battles between players (if a user sets their nickname as public)
  • Displaying ads (monetization through AdMob, Unity Ads, IronSource, AppLovin MAX, and other networks)
  • Game performance and gameplay analytics

5. Matchmaking and battles between players

Players can find others by nickname, but only if that player sets their account to public. No personal or external data is shared — only anonymous UID and public nickname.

6. User rights

Users have the right to:

  • access their data (nickname, game progress),
  • request correction or deletion of their data from Firebase.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: developer@xvoinar.pl Data will be removed within 30 days of the request.
