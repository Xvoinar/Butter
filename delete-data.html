How to Request Data Deletion – Masło Game

Last updated: August 6, 2025

To request deletion of your data from the Masło game, please follow these steps:

Step 1 – Contact Us

Send an email to: developer@xvoinar.pl

Please include:

  • Your in-game nickname (if set)
  • Your Google account email (if you used login)
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request – Masło

Step 2 – Wait for Confirmation

We will respond within 7 business days and delete your game data from our servers.

All associated progress, statistics, and identifiers will be permanently removed from Firebase and related services.

For full details, read our Privacy Policy.
